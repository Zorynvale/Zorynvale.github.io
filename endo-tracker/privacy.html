Privacy Policy - Endo Tracker

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Endo Tracker ("the app") is developed and operated by Zorynvale Digital Inc. ("we", "us", or "our"), an Ontario, Canada corporation. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

In short: Everything you enter into Endo Tracker is stored only on your own device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or have any access to your data. There are no accounts and no servers.

Information you enter

Endo Tracker lets you record health and wellness information so you can track it over time and discuss it with your doctor. This may include:

  • Symptom logs, such as pain level, pain locations, bleeding, period days, energy, mood, and possible triggers
  • Medications you add, such as name, dosage, who prescribed it, frequency, and reminder times
  • Notes and questions for your doctor
  • Optional profile details, such as your name and age, used only to label the Doctor Report
  • Custom symptoms or triggers you choose to add

How your information is stored

All of the information above is stored locally on your device only, using your device’s private app storage. It is never uploaded to us or to any third party. Endo Tracker does not use accounts, cloud storage, or external servers. We have no ability to see, access, or recover your data.

How your information is used

Your information is used solely on your device to provide the app’s features: showing your logs, calendar, trends and patterns, sending the reminders you set up, and generating the Doctor Report. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

Sharing your information

We do not share your information because we never receive it. Information leaves your device only if you choose to:

  • Generate and share a Doctor Report. The report is created on your device and shared only to the app or person you select, for example, email or a messaging app.
  • Export a backup file. You can save a copy of your data to a location you choose. That copy is under your control.

In-app purchases

The Doctor Report can be purchased as an in-app product. Payments are processed by the app store, Google Play or Apple, not by us. We do not receive or store your payment details. Your health data is not sent to the app store as part of a purchase; the report itself is generated on your device.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Endo Tracker schedules local notifications on your device. These reminders are generated and delivered entirely on your device and are not sent through any server.

Third-party services

Endo Tracker does not include advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking. The only third-party service involved is the app store’s billing system, used only if you choose to make a purchase.

Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete individual entries or clear your data at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all data stored on your device. Because we never hold your data, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

Your rights

Since your data stays on your device and under your control, you can view, edit, export, and delete it at any time directly in the app.

Children’s privacy

Endo Tracker is intended for adults and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Medical disclaimer

Endo Tracker is a personal symptom-tracking tool. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition. It does not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your health.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@zorynvale.com.
