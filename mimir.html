Mental health tracker

Mimir

Mental health tracking that turns everyday check-ins into appointment-ready reports.

Mimir is a private, offline-first mental health tracker for people managing their mood, medications, and day-to-day wellbeing. You do a quick daily check-in; Mimir keeps the history and, when it’s time to see your doctor, turns it into a clean, appointment-ready report you can hand over or print. No more trying to remember how the last three months actually went.

Everything stays on your device by default. There’s no account to create and nothing is uploaded, so your health data is yours alone.

Appointment-ready doctor reports

The heart of Mimir. With a couple of taps it generates a professional PDF summarizing your tracked period, so you and your clinician can spend the visit talking instead of reconstructing.

  • Customizable in seconds: choose the date range and check off exactly which sections to include.
  • Clinically organized: mood and sleep trends, medication history, symptom severity, care routines, substance use, and your own notes, laid out clearly.
  • Trend charts your doctor can read at a glance, with markers showing when medications were started or stopped.
  • Questions for your doctor, carried straight into the report so nothing important gets forgotten in the room.
  • Print or share as a standard PDF, or keep a running history under Saved Reports.

Everything else Mimir tracks

  • Daily check-ins for mood, sleep, and the symptoms that matter to you.
  • Medication tracking with reminders and a clear history of changes over time.
  • Symptom logging with severity, so patterns become visible.
  • Trends and insights that reveal how sleep, mood, and medication connect.
  • Safety plan and an “I need help now” screen for quick access to crisis resources when you need them most.
  • Coping toolkit: guided in-the-moment exercises plus printable therapist-style worksheets and handouts.
  • Care tasks to build and keep small daily routines.

Private by design

  • All data stored locally on your device by default.
  • No account, no sign-up, no tracking.
  • App lock with PIN or biometrics to keep your information yours.
  • Encrypted backups you control, so you can move your data without exposing it.
  • Available in English and French.

Mimir is a personal wellbeing tool and is not a medical device. It does not provide diagnosis or treatment and is not a substitute for professional care.
